Twin Cities Esports

Mission Statement

Our mission is to bring a fun and competitive esports league to the twin cities. If there is a game that you like to play we will strive to bring a community together to play. By creating your own partnerships with other players, you'll be able to find out who will play best with you.

Target Audience

My target age would be people both boy and girl, between 16-30 years old. I put it at this because this is a very common age range for people that typically play video games. End users of this website will have had a fun experience playing video games with their friends or with other people on the site.
